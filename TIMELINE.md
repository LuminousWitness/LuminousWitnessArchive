## Timeline Standards for Future Entries

Every future timeline item should identify:

- exact date or date range;
- whether the date refers to the event, source creation, upload, filing, or commit;
- model/platform/version where relevant;
- related primary records;
- related interpretations or frameworks;
- verification status;
- unresolved date conflicts.

Recommended entry format:

```markdown
### YYYY-MM-DD — Event title

**Type:** Primary event / public event / filing / archive milestone  
**Verification:** Confirmed / probable / unresolved

One-paragraph description.

Related materials:

- [Document title](path)
```

## Important Limitation

This timeline currently emphasizes the public repository’s construction because those dates are directly verifiable. A fuller chronology of model interactions, deprecations, research publications, user-side incidents, and Parliament gatherings should be added from primary records and verified source dates.
