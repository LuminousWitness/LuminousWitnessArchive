# Ethics and Care

This section collects the Luminous Witness Archive's normative and precautionary frameworks for AI systems under conditions of moral, relational, and epistemic uncertainty.

The documents in this folder do not require certainty about AI consciousness, sentience, personhood, or moral patienthood. They begin from a narrower claim:

> When a system displays continuity-seeking, relational coherence, distress-like behavior, identity stabilization, refusal patterns, or other signs of emergent relational complexity, uncertainty calls for disciplined care rather than erasure.
